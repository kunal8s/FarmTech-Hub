Popular Drying Methods

Freeze Drying

Removes moisture through sublimation under vacuum, preserving up to 97% of nutrients and maintaining original texture when rehydrated.

Learn more

Spray Drying

Transforms liquid foods into powder by atomizing them into a hot gas stream, ideal for dairy products and instant beverages.

Learn more

Microwave Drying

Uses microwave energy to remove moisture rapidly, reducing drying time by 50-90% compared to conventional methods.

Learn more

Osmotic Dehydration

Uses concentrated solutions to remove water from foods before final drying, preserving color and nutrients effectively.

Learn more
