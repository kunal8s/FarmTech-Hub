Maximizing Nutritional Value

How you store and prepare food significantly impacts its nutritional content. Follow these science-backed methods to preserve nutrients.

Proper Storage

Store leafy greens in airtight containers with paper towels to absorb moisture. Keep tomatoes at room temperature until ripe.

Cooking Methods

Steaming preserves more nutrients than boiling. Quick stir-frying retains crispness and vitamins better than prolonged cooking.

Pairing Foods

Combine vitamin C-rich foods with iron sources to enhance absorption. Add healthy fats to increase uptake of fat-soluble vitamins.

Seasonal Nutrition Guide

Why Eat Seasonally?

Seasonal produce is harvested at peak ripeness, meaning higher nutrient content

Reduced transportation time preserves more vitamins and antioxidants

Natural growing cycles provide nutritional variety throughout the year

Often more flavorful and requires less processing or additives
